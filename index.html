Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the passing percentage?

In most schools in India, the passing marks are 33 out of 100 in each subject.

How is the percentage calculated?

Percentage is calculated by dividing total marks obtained by total maximum marks and multiplying by 100.

Is this calculator accurate?

Yes, it checks all subjects and instantly shows if the student has passed or failed, along with the overall percentage.
